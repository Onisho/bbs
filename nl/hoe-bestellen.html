Hoe bestellen

Volg onderstaande stappen om uw order te plaatsen op bbs.shoes:

Login or register

Use the login button on the left to login or register for a new account. Once verified and logged in, you can start buying shoes.

Choose shoes

Choose the shoes you need, select the prepack and add a number of cartons to your cart. You can add as many as you want.

Your cart

Goto your cart to finalize your order. Check the terms and conditions and place your order.

Delivery address

Add your own reference and choose a delivery address.

Payment method

You can pay securely with VISA, MASTERCARD, MAESTRO or AMERICAN EXPRESS through Ingenico (Ogone), our ISP. We don't keep your creditcard details.

Payment confirmation

You'll be refered to the payment page of our ISP where you can enter your payment details and confirm payment.

Confirmation email

Within a few minutes, you'll receive a confirmation email.
