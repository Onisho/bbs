Payment

How can I pay in a secure way

BBS collaborates with Ingenico, the market leader in Belgium for secure payments on the internet which is approved by Visa and MasterCard. Payment is made in a secure environment. No one can see the data entered, including us. The BBS online shop accepts payment with VISA, MasterCard, Maestro and American Express.

Ingenico

Ingenico is one of the key players in secure online payment. With over 40 available options for payment it offers a wide range of international and local ways of payment. In this way we can offer a secure handling of your online payment with Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Maestro. When finalizing your payment at the BBS e-shop you will be redirected to Ingenico to complete your payment. More information on Ingenico is available at www.ogone.com.
